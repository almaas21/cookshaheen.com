Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 27, 2026

At CookShaheen ("we," "us," or "our"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website cookshaheen.com and interact with our content.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Email Address: When you subscribe to our newsletter or create an account
  • Comments & Feedback: Information you provide when commenting on recipes or contacting us
  • User-Generated Content: Photos, reviews, or recipe variations you share

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, links clicked
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device to improve user experience

2. Pinterest Content Integration

Our Automated Recipe Collection

CookShaheen uses automated technology to curate and share recipes from publicly available sources, including Pinterest. Here's how this works:

  • Public Content Only: We only access and display content that is publicly shared on Pinterest, specifically from our own Pinterest account (@cookshaheen)
  • AI Analysis: We use AI technology (OpenRouter's Molmo) to analyze cooking images and extract recipe information
  • Proper Attribution: All recipes include links back to original sources when available
  • Compliance: Our automated system complies with Pinterest's Terms of Service and API usage policies

2.1 How We Use Pinterest Data

We collect publicly available cooking images and videos from Pinterest to:

  • Analyze and extract recipe information using AI
  • Create detailed, user-friendly recipe posts on our website
  • Provide our community with diverse cooking inspiration
  • Maintain a curated collection of quality recipes

2.2 Your Pinterest Content

If you are a Pinterest user and believe your content has been used inappropriately:

  • We respect copyright and intellectual property rights
  • Content is only sourced from public pins and boards
  • You can request removal of any content by contacting us (see Section 9)
  • We will respond to removal requests within 48 hours

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our website and services
  • Send you newsletters, recipe updates, and cooking tips (with your consent)
